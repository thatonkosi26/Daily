About Daily

At Daily, we're passionate about simplifying your daily routines. Our platform is designed to seamlessly integrate task management with essential weather insights and fashion advice. Whether you're planning your day or optimizing your wardrobe, Daily is here to enhance your everyday productivity and style.

To-Do List

Stay organized and in control of your day. The to-do list lets you easily add, update, complete, or delete tasks. Whether it's a quick errand or a long-term goal, Daily helps you prioritize and manage your responsibilities with a clean, user-friendly interface. Tasks are stored so you can track your progress and build consistent habits.

Real-Time Weather Updates

Plan your day with accurate, live weather data. Daily integrates real-time weather information based on your location, so you're always prepared for what's outside. Whether it's sunny, raining, or chilly, you get instant updates that help inform your activities and clothing choices throughout the day.

Outfit Recommendations

Dress smart, every time. Based on the current weather conditions, Daily suggests outfit ideas tailored to temperature, wind, humidity, and conditions like rain or sunshine. No more over- or under-dressing—Daily helps you step out feeling comfortable and confident.

AI Assistance

Your personal productivity partner. Powered by AI, Daily can assist with task suggestions, time estimates, productivity tips, or simply answer your questions. Whether you’re feeling overwhelmed or unsure where to start, your AI assistant offers smart suggestions and guidance to keep you moving forward.

FAQs

What is Daily?

Daily is a smart, all-in-one personal assistant web app that combines a to-do list, weather updates, outfit recommendations, and AI support to help you plan and manage your day effortlessly.

How does the to-do list work?

The to-do list allows you to add, update, and complete tasks. It helps you prioritize and stay on track with your responsibilities throughout the day.

Where does Daily get weather updates from?

Daily uses a trusted weather API to fetch accurate, real-time weather data based on your current location.

How are outfit recommendations made?

Based on the current temperature, weather conditions, and season, Daily suggests clothing ideas that are comfortable and suitable for the day.

What can the AI assistant do?

The AI assistant can help you with productivity tips, suggest task prioritization, answer general questions, and give motivational boosts to keep you focused.

Is Daily free to use?

Yes! Daily is free to use with essential features. Premium features may be introduced in the future for an enhanced experience.

Do I need to create an account?

While some features can be explored without an account, signing up gives you full access to personalized tasks, saved preferences, and AI recommendations.

Can I access Daily on mobile?

Absolutely! Daily is designed to be mobile-responsive so you can manage your day from any device—phone, tablet, or desktop.

Is my data safe on Daily?

Yes, your data is securely stored using Firebase and is only accessible by you. We prioritize privacy and data protection.

How can I give feedback or request a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! You can use the contact form on the website or email us directly to share your thoughts and feature ideas.
